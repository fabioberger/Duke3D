A LITTLE ABOUT DUKE MAKERS

Duke Makers is committed to bringing, developing, and incorporating 3D printing best practices and processes on campus wherever it can. It aspires to inspire all generations of Duke students with the next generation of manufacturing and product design. It seeks to work with people across Duke's disciplines (Mathematics, Engineering, Biology/Medical, and Visual Arts) to bring a better living and working experience to Duke's campus, to improve the lives of faculty, staff, and students, and to raise Duke's capability to reach and touch people's lives worldwide. Duke Makers is a student organization on campus. It will be comprised of three main parts:

Engineers/Computer Scientists/Mathematicians who build/write/maintain software and hardware for the printers/accessories/websites, Visual Arts/Modellers who create/refine models for use by Duke students and others, and an Outreach/Faculty Relations/Student Gov relations/Admin relations group to ensure that 3D printing club will become/remain a core part of Duke life. It combines all enthusiasts, associated entrepreneurs, and interested students under one umbrella to give them both a voice and a means to advance the progress of 3D printing on campus
